Meet Dr. V. S. Rathore

MBBS , MS – General Surgery ,
MCh – Plastic Surgery

Dr. Rathore, the best hair transplant surgeon in Kolkata, has been focused on helping and developing the well-being of his patients for almost two decades now. He has been completely involved in carefully and precisely calculating the procedures required to achieve and meet his patient’s wants, and boost their self-confidence for more than 18 years.  He has performed more than 9300 successful surgeries and more than 2100 hair transplants.

Dr. Rathore was inspired to practice as a surgeon after seeing different patients suffering from various kinds of trauma, who find it very difficult to get surgery. He attended Kolkata Medical College where he pursued his Master of Surgery degree in general surgery and M.Ch. in plastic surgery. He is also a former professor of Medical College and Hospital, Kolkata.
